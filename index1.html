    Know Your Nation:NIGERIA

    This book "Know Your Country: Nigeria" provides a broad overview of the people of Nigeria. It recognises the past leaders who helped in gaining the country's independence in 1960 from British colonial masters. This book is as a result of vigorous extensive research by the authors to provide an in-depth knowledge about the uniqueness in each state of the federation.

    Each chapter reflects the rich culture of each state through their leadership styles, mode of greeting and dressing, tourist sites, delicacies and festivals. At the end of each chapter are some common names and their English meaning.

    As Nigerians or Nigerian living in diaspora with little or no knowledge about your country, this book aims at providing you with useful information about your root.
